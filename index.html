"This project uses Tableau to visualize bike parts sales across Europe, focusing on sales insights, profit analysis, and yearly trends."

Project Title: Analysis of Bike Parts Sales Across European Countries
Objective: Sales Insight per Country and State: To extract and analyze the sales data of bike parts across various countries and their respective states in Europe. Profit Analysis: To calculate and visualize the sum and average profit made from bike part sales in each European country. Yearly Sales Trends: To track and visualize the yearly sales trends of bike parts in each country, providing insights into sales performance over time.
Technical Details: Tools Used: Tableau: Used for data visualization, specifically to create:
Bar Chart: For visualizing the sum and average profit made from bike part sales for each country.
Map: For geographical visualization of sales data across different countries and states in Europe.
Line Chart: For displaying the sales of bike parts per year by each country, highlighting trends and patterns in sales over time. By utilizing Tableau for data visualization, the project provides a comprehensive and visually engaging analysis of bike parts sales in Europe, offering valuable insights for stakeholders to make informed decisions.
